## About Me

I'm a PhD Candidate in **Data Science and Computation** at the University of Bologna, focused on **AI-based Optimization of Large-Scale AI Workloads** in High-Performance Computing Systems.

- Profiling and benchmarking **GenAI training and inference workloads** on HPC infrastructures
- Investigating optimization strategies (parallelism, quantization, batching) on **heterogeneous accelerators**
- Developing AI-driven methods for **HPC workload management**: predictive resource allocation, job scheduling, energy-aware monitoring
- Open to collaborating on **AI open-source projects** of any kind
